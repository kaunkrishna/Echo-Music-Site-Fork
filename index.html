Do I need a YouTube Music subscription?

+

No! Echo Music streams music directly from YouTube Music's free tier. You don't need any paid subscriptions to use the app. The app bypasses ads and provides additional features like offline playback and synced lyrics.

What Android versions are supported?

+

Echo Music supports Android 5.0 (API level 21) and above. This covers most Android devices from 2014 onwards. The app is optimized for modern Android versions but maintains compatibility with older devices.

Can I download music for offline listening?

+

Yes! Echo Music supports offline playback. You can download songs, albums, and playlists to listen without an internet connection. Downloaded content is stored locally on your device and can be managed through the app's download manager.

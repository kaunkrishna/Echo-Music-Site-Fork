General & Interface

How do I change the app's theme or colors?

+

Go to Settings > Interface. You can toggle between Dark, Light, or Follow System modes. For OLED displays, you can enable Pure black. You can also enable the Dynamic theme (Material You) to match your system colors, and even customize the Player background style and Player button colors directly.

What is "Echo Brain" and how do I use it?

+

Echo Brain is an experimental AI recommendation engine that learns your listening habits, maps discovered genres, and injects AI recommendations. You can enable it in Settings. You also have full control over your Neural Profile, with options to export, import, or completely reset it.

Can I customize the player controls and navigation?

+

Yes. In Settings > Interface, you can customize the navigation tabs (hide or show Home, Music, Playlists, etc.), change the Player slider style (Default, Apple Music, or Wavy), and tweak the Mini-player's appearance.

Tools & Features

How do I use Music Recognition?

+

Echo Music includes a built-in Music Recognizer. You can add the "Recognize music" tile to your Android Quick Settings panel. Tapping it will listen to your surroundings to identify the playing song, which you can then instantly listen to on Echo Music.

How do I import my music from Spotify?

+

In the Settings menu under Integrations, you can connect your Spotify account. From there, you can choose playlists (including your Liked Songs) and mirror them directly into local Echo Music playlists. You can also import any public Spotify playlist simply by pasting its link!

Player & Audio

What is Audio Offload? Should I enable it?

+

Audio Offload uses a dedicated, hardware-accelerated audio path for playback which can save battery life. However, it may cause issues with certain post-processing effects on some devices, and is automatically disabled if you turn on Crossfade.

Does Echo Music support Gapless playback and Crossfade?

+

Yes! You can enable Crossfade in Settings > Player & Audio, and customize the crossfade duration. There is also an option to "Disable for gapless albums" to ensure gapless albums play seamlessly.

What is "Skip Silence"?

+

Enable "Skip silence" in the Player settings to automatically fast-forward through silent parts of songs. You can also select "Instantly skip silence" to jump ahead instead of just speeding up playback.

Does Echo Music have an Equalizer?

+

Yes, Echo Music includes a professional-grade Equalizer with Simple and Advanced modes. It features Acoustic tuning, Bass Boost, custom profiles, Dirac/Dolby presets, and system equalizer support. Find it in Settings > Player & Audio > Equalizer.

Lyrics & Content

How do I get translated lyrics?

+

Echo Music supports AI Lyrics Translation. In Settings > Integrations, you can configure an AI provider, enter an API key, and select a Target Language to translate or transcribe lyrics automatically.

What is Lyrics Romanization?

+

Romanization converts lyrics from scripts like Japanese, Korean, Cyrillic, and more into the Latin alphabet, making them easier to read. You can enable specific languages for romanization in the settings and choose if you want them displayed as the primary lyrics.

Can I change the lyrics animation style?

+

Absolutely. Echo Music offers multiple word-by-word animation styles including Fade, Glow, Slide, Karaoke, Apple Music, and Vivi Music (Fluid). You can also adjust lyrics text size, line spacing, and blur effects for inactive lines.

Privacy & Integrations

How does "Listen Together" work?

+

Listen Together lets you listen to music with friends in real-time. You can create a room as a host and share the code, or join an existing room. Go to Settings > Integrations > Listen Together to configure the Server URL and your Username.

Can I scrobble to Last.fm or ListenBrainz?

+

Yes. Connect your Last.fm and ListenBrainz accounts in Settings > Integrations to automatically track your listening history. You can even configure scrobble delays and minimum track durations.

Does the app track my listening history?

+

Your listen and search history are stored purely locally on your device. You can pause or completely clear your history at any time under Settings > Privacy & Security.

Storage & Downloads

How do downloads and offline playback work?

+

You can download playlists, albums, or individual songs. There is also an "Auto download on like" feature in Settings > Storage & Data that automatically saves any track you like for offline playback.

Can I export my music as MP3?

+

Yes. Echo Music allows you to export downloaded music. First, you must set an "Export folder" in the Storage settings. Afterwards, you can use the Export option on songs to save them locally to your device with embedded metadata.

My device is running out of space. What should I do?

+

Navigate to Settings > Storage & Data. Here, you can limit the "Max song cache size" and "Max image cache size". You can also manually clear the song cache, image cache, or remove downloaded app updates to free up space instantly.
